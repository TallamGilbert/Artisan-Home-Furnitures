Our Story

For over 20 years, Artisan Home has been crafting premium furniture with a focus on quality materials, timeless design, and local craftsmanship.

Our team of skilled artisans combines traditional woodworking techniques with modern design principles to create furniture that stands the test of time.

We're proud to be a local business supporting local communities, with all our furniture made in our workshops using sustainably sourced materials.
